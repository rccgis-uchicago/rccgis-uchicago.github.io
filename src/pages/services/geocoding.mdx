## Data Preparation

### Required Format
<div className="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-1 gap-6 my-6">
  <div className="border rounded-lg p-4">
    <h4 className="font-semibold mb-2">File Specifications</h4>
    <ul className="space-y-2">
      <li>Format: CSV (comma-separated value)</li>
      <li>Encoding: UTF-8</li>
      <li>Headers: Required (standardized)</li>
      <li>Size: Up to 50,000 records per batch</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>


### Accepted Column Headers
<div className="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-6 my-6">
  <div>
    <ul className="space-y-2">
      <li><code>ID</code>: Unique identifier</li>
      <li><code>ADDRESS</code>: Street address</li>
      <li><code>NEIGHBORHOOD</code>: Local area name</li>
      <li><code>CITY</code>: City name</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div>
    <ul className="space-y-2">
      <li><code>SUBREGION</code>: County/district</li>
      <li><code>REGION/STATE/ST</code>: State/province</li>
      <li><code>POSTAL/ZIP/ZIPCODE</code>: Postal code</li>
      <li><code>COUNTRYCODE</code>: ISO country code</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

### 3. Understanding Results

<div className="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-6 my-6">
  <div className="border rounded-lg p-4">
    <h4 className="font-semibold mb-2">Output Fields</h4>
    <ul className="space-y-2">
      <li><strong>LATITUDE</strong>: Coordinate position</li>
      <li><strong>LONGITUDE</strong>: Coordinate position</li>
      <li><strong>MATCH_SCORE</strong>: Accuracy indicator</li>
      <li><strong>MATCH_TYPE</strong>: Level of match</li>
      <li><strong>MATCH_ADDR</strong>: Matched address</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div className="border rounded-lg p-4">
    <h4 className="font-semibold mb-2">Match Score Guide</h4>
    <ul className="space-y-2">
      <li><span className="text-green-600">100%</span>: Perfect match</li>
      <li><span className="text-yellow-600">90-99%</span>: Suitable for most uses</li>
      <li><span className="text-red-600">&lt;90%</span>: Review recommended</li>
    </ul>

  </div>
</div>
