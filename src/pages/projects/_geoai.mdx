## Petra Water Management Project

### Overview
Advanced spatial analysis of ancient water and agricultural systems in Petra's hinterlands, combining:
- Aerial photogrammetry
- Terrestrial LiDAR scanning
- Historical data integration

### Research Findings
The project challenges conventional views about infrastructure development in Petra by:
- Revealing bottom-up water management systems
- Documenting local agricultural practices
- Mapping infrastructure networks
- Analyzing sustainability patterns

### Modern Applications
- Contemporary land use planning
- Water resource management
- Heritage preservation strategies
- Sustainable development insights
